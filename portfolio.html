About Me

I am a purpose-driven and service-oriented human services professional with a Post Graduate Diploma in Human Services focused on Children and Families from Southeastern University and a Bachelor of Arts in Social Development from Makerere University.

My professional journey reflects a deep commitment to strengthening families, protecting children, and supporting communities through compassionate service, leadership development, and practical training. I believe in creating lasting impact through structured systems, clear processes, and values-driven leadership.

Guided by strong spiritual convictions and a nurturing yet firm approach, I strive to balance care with accountability. I am naturally drawn to building frameworks, training programs, and tools that equip others to create safe, nurturing environments where children can flourish.

Nurturing Leaders, Strengthening Families, Transforming Communities

Safe Steps Training Services is a consultancy and family support organization I founded to develop responsible leaders, strengthen families, and support communities in creating safe, nurturing environments. We equip leaders, caregivers, institutions, and communities with mentorship, practical tools, and guidance that foster resilience, integrity, and compassion.

Our Approach

  • Mentorship & Leadership Formation: Empowering youth, parents, and community leaders to guide others with integrity and wisdom.
  • Family Strengthening: Equipping families with skills, emotional support, and practical resources to create stable, nurturing homes.
  • Child Safeguarding & Development: Ensuring safe environments through training, policy support, and structured child-focused programs.
